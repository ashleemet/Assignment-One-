ABOUT ME
My name is Ashlee Metcalfe and I currently am currently a first year Design student at Massey. Design and art has been a passion of mine some way or another, from a very young age. While in primary school, inspired by ‘Captain Underpants‘ I created a comic book series called 'Super Cat'. By the time I was in intermediate, I had developed a love for photography. With my little Fujifilm digital camera – I felt like a professional. My passion for design however, really started during High School. I began to create strange things on photoshop, learning how to navigate through trial and error (and a bit of YouTube.) I thoughrougly enjoyed my Design class at school, and decided to continue my study for it at a higher level at Massey University.
